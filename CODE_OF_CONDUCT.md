# Code of Conduct

Ground Zero is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

## Our standards
Expected behavior includes:
- Treating everyone with respect and professionalism
- Giving constructive feedback with clear technical context
- Assuming positive intent while discussing ideas
- Helping others learn and improve

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Discrimination based on identity or background
- Spam, scams, doxxing, or sharing malicious content
- Repeated disruptive behavior after warnings

## Reporting
If you experience or witness a violation, open a private discussion with maintainers or report through organization channels with:
- What happened
- Where and when it happened
- Any relevant links or evidence

## Enforcement
Maintainers may take actions including warning, content removal, temporary restriction, or permanent removal from community spaces.

## Scope
This code applies across Ground Zero repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and community spaces linked from the organization.
