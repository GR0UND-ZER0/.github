# Security Policy

Ground Zero takes security reports seriously and appreciates responsible disclosure.

## Supported scope
This policy applies to active repositories under the Ground Zero organization.

## How to report a vulnerability
Please do not open public issues for vulnerabilities.

Instead, report privately with:
- Repository and affected area
- Steps to reproduce
- Impact assessment
- Proof of concept (if available)

## Response expectations
- Initial acknowledgement target: within 72 hours
- Status updates provided as investigation progresses
- Coordinated disclosure after remediation when possible

## Out of scope
The following are generally out of scope unless they lead to meaningful impact:
- Social engineering without technical exploit path
- Denial-of-service test traffic
- Reports without reproducible details

Thank you for helping keep Ground Zero secure.
